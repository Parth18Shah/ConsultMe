Welcome to ConsultMe

The lockdown that took place due to the Covid-19 all around the country had some severe impact on people. Most of them were afraid to go outside of their homes. People were reluctant to go even for a routine checkup.

The aim of this project is to provide an effective solution for the same. The patient has to enter the symptoms and then our Machine-Learning based Web Application will update them about the disease which they are suffering from and suggest some home remedies and treatments for the same.

"An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure "

Predict a Disease

User can enter the symptoms and get to know about the disease

Positive Feedbacks for Predicted Disease

Consult a Doctor

User can consult with a doctor online using our website
